EMPLOYMENT APPLICATION FORM



The ministry of Education and other stakeholder is conducting an enumeration exercise in various counties that have been select. The aim is to take number of student who did their KCSE for the past two years and are yet to join colleges/universities due finacial issues. In partnership with the Equity Bank's Wing's to Fly program, there is a plan to enrol the need students to various technical institutions for them to acquire technical skills that will reduce unemployment level through self employment and job creation in various sectors. We a looking to hire forty enumerators who will conduct house to house interviews targeting the started population. the first rollout exercise will be conducted in three counties:

  1. Machakos county
  2. Nairobi county
  3. Kiambu county
  4. Nakuru county

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent: A high school diploma or equivalent is often the minimum requirement.
